### 主语一致
0. 构造两个主语一致的简单句
1. 选定其中一句话做状语
2. 省略作状语的句子主语
3. 被省略的主语后的动词变成 v+ing（如果是being可以省略）

### e.g. 做完家庭作业，我就回家了。

|---| 从句 |主句 | 
|---|---|---|
|简单句（相同主语）|I <H2>had finished my</H2> homework.|I went home.|
|状语从句（5.5—6）|After I <H2>had finished</H2> my homework,|I went home.|
|简化为介词短语作状语|After <H2>having</H2> finished my homework,|I went home.|
|精简后的分词从句|<H2>Having finished</H2> my home work,|I went home.|

## 非谓语动词表示被动

### 原因状语从句

#### Because 先哭还是先被打不明

| 从句 |主句 | 
|---|---|
|Because the boy <H1>was beaten</H1> by a girl,|the boy cried.|
|Because <H1>being beaten</H1> by a girl,|the boy cried.|
|<H1>Beaten</H1> by a girl,|the boy cried.|

| 从句 |主句 | 
|---|---|
|I was very thirsty.|I drank up all the water in the bottle.|
|Because I was very thirsty,|I drank up all the water in the bottle.|
|(Being) Very thirsty,|I drank up all the water in the bottle.|

| 从句 |主句 | 
|---|---|
|I was sick. |I stayed at home.|
|Because I was sick,|I stayed at home.|
|Being sick,|I stayed at home.|

### 时间状语从句

#### After 先被打后哭

| 从句 |主句 | 
|---|---|

|After <H1>having been beaten</H1> by a girl,|the boy cried.|
|<H1>Having been beaten</H1> by a girl,|the boy cried.|
